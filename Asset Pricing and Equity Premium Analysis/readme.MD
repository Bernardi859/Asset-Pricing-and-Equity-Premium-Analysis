\## Repository Contents



\- \*\*`Report.pdf`\*\* – A brief summary of the project, including methodology, key results, and discussion of the equity premium puzzle.  

\- \*\*`TFPdata.xlsx`\*\* – Raw and processed U.S. quarterly TFP data (source: FRBSF). Contains quarterly, annual, and capital‑input details.Publicly available at \[Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco - Total Factor Productivity (TFP) Data](https://www.frbsf.org/research-and-insights/data-and-indicators/total-factor-productivity-tfp/)-

\- \*\*`code.ipynb`\*\* Jupyter notebook with full reproducible code:  

&#x20; - AR(1) estimation of TFP growth  

&#x20; - Rouwenhorst discretization (9 states)  

&#x20; - Value function iteration for a stochastic neoclassical growth model  

&#x20; - Simulation of 10,000 quarters and computation of asset pricing moments (risk‑free rate, equity return, equity premium)
